No Stupid Questions

A blog dedicated to the most trivial applications of science

"If you give a man an answer and he’ll have a little fact, but if you give a man a question then hell go find his own answers."
- Kvothe

I think the best way to learn is to ask smart people dumb questions. Unfortunately, I don’t know any smart people so I have to make do with asking myself silly questions. This blog tackles questions of all magnitudes, anything from spiderman maximum jump height to the power loss caused by your phone case when you wirelessly charge it.

Just so you know, I am not the first person to have this idea and if you like this and want to read something in a similar vein written by someone way smarter than me then, please to check out Randle Monroes' "What if?"
